# STRATECH

## Company Overview

STRATECH is a software consulting company focused on designing and developing custom digital solutions for enterprise clients across multiple industries.

Projects ranged from banking and retail analytics to interactive customer experiences, requiring multidisciplinary engineering across backend services, frontend applications, cloud infrastructure, DevOps, and artificial intelligence integrations.

Working as part of a consulting company required quickly understanding different business domains, adapting to existing architectures, and delivering production-ready software for clients with diverse technical requirements.

---

## My Role

Full Stack Software Engineer

During my time at STRATECH, I worked across multiple enterprise projects, contributing to backend development, frontend applications, cloud integrations, deployment automation, infrastructure, and software architecture.

My responsibilities evolved depending on the client and project, allowing me to gain experience across several industries while working with different technologies and engineering challenges.

---

## Main Projects

### BBVA

Customer flow management platform used nationwide across BBVA branches.

The platform combines queue management, customer prioritization, computer vision, and operational analytics.

---

### Farmacias del Ahorro

Retail analytics platform used by interactive smart displays installed in pharmacies.

The platform collected customer interaction data, computer vision analytics, offline synchronization, and operational dashboards.

---

### IFIT

Interactive product exploration platform deployed on large touch displays.

The application dynamically generated complete interactive experiences from JSON configuration files, allowing the same frontend to support multiple installations without code duplication.
