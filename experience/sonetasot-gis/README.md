# SONETASOT

## Company Overview

SONETASOT is a software development company specialized in building digital platforms for government institutions and public organizations.

The company develops custom software solutions focused on geographic information systems (GIS), content management systems (CMS), digital transformation, and public information platforms.

Projects typically involve modernizing legacy systems, improving security, automating operational processes, and building platforms that can be maintained by non-technical users.

## Main Projects

### Metropolitan GIS Platform

A public Geographic Information System (GIS) that provides interactive access to more than 500 geographic layers covering the Guadalajara Metropolitan Area.

The platform integrates Django, GeoServer, and OpenLayers to manage, publish, and visualize spatial information while maintaining synchronization between application data and GIS services.

---

### Government CMS Migration Platform

A custom Content Management System built with Django and Wagtail to replace legacy WordPress websites used by government institutions.

The platform allows non-technical users to create and manage complete websites while supporting automated migration of existing pages, news articles, media assets, and other legacy content.
