# SONETASOT CMS

## Company Overview

SONETASOT developed digital platforms for public institutions, focusing on modern web applications, content management systems, and digital transformation projects.

One of the company's major initiatives involved replacing legacy CMS platforms with modern, secure, and maintainable solutions capable of supporting multiple institutional websites from a single platform.

Projects emphasized long-term maintainability, content migration, security, and usability for non-technical users.

## My Role

**Full Stack Software Engineer**

During my time at SONETASOT, I contributed to both frontend and backend development while progressively taking on technical leadership responsibilities.

My work included developing CMS modules, implementing backend services, automating large-scale content migrations, improving user experience, mentoring junior developers, and participating directly in production deployments.

## Main Project

### Government CMS Migration Platform

[cms-platform.md](cms-platform.md)

A custom content management platform built with Django and Wagtail to replace legacy WordPress installations used by multiple government websites.

The platform enabled non-technical users to create, manage, and publish complete websites while supporting automated migration of existing content.
