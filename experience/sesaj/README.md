# Company Overview

## Overview

The Executive Secretariat of the Jalisco State Anti-Corruption System (SESAJ) develops and operates digital platforms that enable interoperability among public institutions, securely managing the information, credentials, and integrations required to comply with the National Digital Platform.

During my tenure I designed and developed key components of the ecosystem, taking part in decisions on architecture, security, monitoring, and infrastructure.

---

# Business Context

The main challenge was to securely manage information from multiple public entities, streamline their onboarding into the National Digital Platform, and continuously verify that their services remained available and met the established technical standards.

The platform had to support multiple organizations, heterogeneous government systems, and steady growth in the number of integrations.

---

# My Role

Backend Lead / Full Stack Engineer

My primary responsibility was to design and implement technical solutions for the evolution of the ecosystem—defining architecture, service-to-service communication, security, deployments, and new backend and frontend features.

I also took part in infrastructure decisions, deployment automation, and the design of institutional APIs.

---

# Main Projects

## Bóveda

Platform for the secure administration of institutional secrets, organizations, and interconnections, enabling controlled and auditable credential sharing.

---

## Centinela

Continuous monitoring platform responsible for validating the availability and interoperability of services exposed by public entities, running automated checks and producing operational metrics.
