# Engineering Profile

## Overview

I am a Senior Full Stack Software Engineer with a strong specialization in backend architecture, distributed systems, cloud infrastructure, and enterprise platform development.

Throughout my career I have participated in the design and implementation of cloud-native platforms, interoperability systems, Geographic Information Systems (GIS), enterprise Content Management Systems (CMS), SaaS platforms, monitoring systems, and data-intensive applications for both government institutions and private companies.

Although I have worked across the entire software stack, my primary expertise lies in designing backend architectures, defining service boundaries, building REST APIs, automating cloud infrastructure, and creating maintainable software platforms capable of evolving over time.

I enjoy solving complex engineering problems by designing systems that are secure, scalable, maintainable, and easy to extend.

---

# Engineering Philosophy

I believe software engineering is primarily about solving business problems rather than writing code.

Technology is only a tool.

My approach focuses on understanding the problem first, defining clear architectural boundaries, minimizing unnecessary complexity, and selecting technologies that best fit the long-term needs of the project.

Whenever possible I prioritize:

- Clear system boundaries.
- Single responsibility.
- Security by design.
- Maintainability.
- Automation.
- Scalability.
- Simplicity.

I prefer designing systems that remain understandable years after they are built.
